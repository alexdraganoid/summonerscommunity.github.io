Hall of Fame

The Hall of Fame is reserved for the most active members of our community. To become a top contributor, all you need to do is be active in the group (posting & commenting) while following the rules. Every contributor can choose to promote one platform of his liking. Also, this document will be pinned on the top for the next month. For now, check out the top contributors for the previous month.

What we do

In this group there is a wide variety of activities to do such as promote streams and YouTube channels, discussing various League related topics, seeking help/advice. Additionally, you can also use this group to promote (once a day) your YouTube channel or Twitch stream, even share YouTube videos that you created. However, during the event "Off Topic Friday" there are no limitations. You can post/talk about anything you like, as long as it does not break the rules. Finally, don't forget to join our Discord

About

Welcome, fellow summoner in YOUR community! The group contains gamers from all regions and divisions. Our point is to create the most friendly and reliable environment for EVERY 'League of Legends' player. If you want to be a member here, you need to know and follow both rules of our group and Summoner's Code.
